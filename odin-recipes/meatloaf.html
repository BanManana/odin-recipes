Basic Meatloaf Recipe

Ingredients that you will need:

  • 2 lbs. Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 Yellow Onion Diced
  • 3/4 C Ketchup
  • 1/3 C Brown Sugar
  • 1 C Breadcrumbs
  • 1 Egg
  • Salt & Pepper to Taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat Oven to 400°F.
  2. Mix Ground Beef, Egg, 1/2 C Ketchup, Breadcrumbs, and seasonings in a bowl.
  3. Form loaf in pan or use loaf pan.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes covered in foil.
  5. Take reserve Ketchup and Brown Sugar, mix for topping.
  6. Top Meatloaf with mixture, bake uncovered for an additional 15 minutes or until internal temperature is 165°F.
  7. Yell at Refs in Hawkeye's Game and enjoy.
