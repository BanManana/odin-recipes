White People Taco Night Tacos

Welcome to your new favorite Midwestern Caucasian Taco Recipe

Ingredients that you will need:

  • 1 lb. Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 packet of Taco Seasoning
  • 1 Yellow Onion Diced
  • Taco Shells
  • Lettuce Shredded
  • extra toppings

Instructions:

  1. Brown Ground Beef in pan on Medium-High Heat and Drain. Return to pan.
  2. Add Taco Seasoning and 1 cup of water to pan of browned ground beef. Bring to boil, simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Portion taco meat with diced onion and shredded lettuce into taco shells with additional toppings.
  4. Listen to Willie Nelson and enjoy.
